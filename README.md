## Hi, I'm Sherzodkhuja 👋

I'm a developer who likes turning ideas into real, finished products — not
half-working demos. I focus on **front-end and product engineering**: clean
interfaces, smooth UX, and apps that feel polished enough to actually hand to
someone. When a project needs a backend, an API, or some Python on the side, I
build that too.

A few things that are true across everything I make:

- 🎨 **I sweat the front-end.** Animations, glassmorphism, haptics,
  keyboard-aware layouts — the details that make an app feel good to use.
- 🔒 **Privacy & local-first by default.** Several of my apps run entirely on
  your own machine, with no telemetry and no data leaving the device.
- 🌍 **Built for real people, in their language.** I ship multilingual UIs
  (Uzbek, English, Russian, Japanese, Spanish) almost by reflex.
- 🤖 **I like wiring AI into products** — vision models, speech-to-text,
  translation — when it actually solves a problem.

I learn by building. My repos range from a mobile bill-splitter with AI receipt
scanning, to a real-time desktop speech translator, to a webcam gesture
controller for PC audio.
